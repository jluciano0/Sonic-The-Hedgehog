Use the left and right parts of the control pad to move sonic left and right.


Use A B or C to make Sonic Jump. Move while jumping to make him jump horizontally.


Use up and down on the control pad to make sonic look up or crouch.


While moving, crouch to attack enemies.


Press start to pause the game.
